How It Works

Specifys.ai empowers you to transform your app idea into reality with our AI-driven platform, supporting vibe coding for a creative and intuitive development flow. Choose one of three tailored tracks to create a detailed specification, build a technical infrastructure, or conduct market research for your project.

Non-Programmers

Perfect for people without coding experience who want a clear specification for a fully coded app to share with developers.

  • Describe your app idea in simple terms.
  • Answer guided questions about features and users.
  • Receive a user-friendly specification for a coded app.
  • Export as PDF to share with developers.
Start Non-Programmers Track

Developers

Designed for coders who need a detailed technical infrastructure to jumpstart their app development.

  • Input your app idea with technical preferences.
  • Answer questions about stack, APIs, and architecture.
  • Get a comprehensive specification with file structure and scripts.
  • Export to coding platforms (Replit, Gitpod) or local setup.
Start Developers Track

Entrepreneurs

Ideal for visionaries who want to validate their app idea with in-depth market research.

  • Share your app concept and target market.
  • Answer questions about competition and goals.
  • Receive a detailed market research report.
  • Export as PDF to pitch to investors or stakeholders.
Start Entrepreneurs Track
