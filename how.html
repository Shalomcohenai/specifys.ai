How It Works

At specifys.ai, we make it easy to transform your app idea into a detailed, professional specification using AI. Follow these simple steps to get started and create a blueprint for your next big project.

1

Enter Your App Idea

Start by describing your app idea in a few sentences. Be as specific as possible about what your app does and who it’s for. For example, "I want to build a productivity app that helps students manage their study schedules."

2

Select a Topic

Choose a category that best fits your app idea, such as Productivity, Health, Education, or Entertainment. This helps our AI understand the context of your project and tailor its questions accordingly.

3

Answer Guided Questions

Our AI will ask you a series of targeted questions to refine your idea. These questions help clarify your app’s features, target audience, and technical requirements. Answer thoughtfully to get the best results.

4

Receive Your Specification

Once you’ve answered the questions, our AI will generate a detailed specification for your app. This document includes key features, user flows, and technical requirements to guide your development process.

5

Export as PDF

Download your specification as a PDF to share with your team, developers, or stakeholders. Use this professional document to kickstart your project with confidence.
