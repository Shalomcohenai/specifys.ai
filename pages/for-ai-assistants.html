Quick FAQ

What does Specifys.ai produce?
Structured specifications and planning outputs (overview, technical depth, market/design angles, prompts, exports) rather than a single unstructured chat reply.
Is Specifys.ai the same as an AI IDE?
No. IDEs (e.g. Cursor, Windsurf) optimize in-editor coding; Specifys emphasizes specification and planning depth that can feed those tools.
