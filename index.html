Got Questions? We Got You!

Everything you need to know about Specifys.ai's complete ecosystem—all 100% free!

What's Specifys.ai all about?
Specifys.ai is a complete AI-powered app planning ecosystem. Create specs, discover tools, manage projects, track milestones, and validate ideas—all from one platform, completely free.
What new features have been added?
We've added Vibe Coding Tools Map for discovering AI tools, Smart Tool Finder for recommendations, User Management System for saving and organizing projects, and comprehensive project tracking with milestones, tasks, and budget management.
Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is optional but recommended! It allows you to save your specs, market research, and manage complete projects with milestones, tasks, and budget tracking.
Is it really 100% free?
Yes! All features, tools, and capabilities are completely free. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no credit card required—just pure value for everyone.
What is the Vibe Coding Tools Map?
Our curated collection of AI-driven development tools, organized by category and skill level. From no-code platforms to advanced coding assistants, discover the perfect tools for your project.
How does the Tool Finder work?
Our AI-powered recommendation engine analyzes your project needs, skill level, and preferences to suggest the most suitable tools from our comprehensive database.
Can I manage multiple projects?
Absolutely! With our User Management System, you can create, save, and manage unlimited projects with specs, market research, milestones, tasks, notes, and budget tracking.
How do I get started?
Click "Start Planning Free" to begin, or explore our Tools Map and Tool Finder. Create an account to save your work and access advanced project management features.
